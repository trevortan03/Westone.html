Westone W10

Description

A single balanced armature driver delivers high fidelity sound reproduction. Using the same advanced technology found in Westone’s iconic ES Series, the W10 delivers extraordinary detail and clarity.

Westone True Fit Technology

50+ years experience with in-ear applications has yielded a lowprofile, lightweight, universal earpiece which delivers maximum comfort and in-ear coupling for dynamic transfer of sound.

Replaceable cable

New replaceable cable connection includes the braided epic cable and round MFI cable with 3-button control and mic.

Balanced Armature

Westone’s balanced armature drivers are significantly more compact and efficient than traditional dynamic drivers. By combining multiple balanced armature drivers with sophisticated crossover networks, Westone monitors provide enhanced sonic detail and frequency range that extends well beyond typical in-ear solutions.

Exchangeable Faceplate

Black, blue and red color faceplates with secure attachment.

Monitor Vault

R-Series earphones ship with Westone’s new Monitor Vault. Designed specifically for transporting high-end earphones and manufactured in Colorado of a highimpact polymer, it’s the perfect size for travel and storage. Each Monitor Vault features a protective foam interior and weather resistant design.
